Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze e-commerce sales data to
understand revenue trends, top-performing products, customer behavior,
and country-wise performance to support data-driven business decisions.

Dataset
Online Retail / E-commerce Orders Dataset
Columns include Invoice No, Stock Code, Description, Quantity,
Invoice Date, Unit Price, Customer ID, and Country.

Tools Used
- Tableau

Key KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Quantity Sold
- Total Customers

Key Insights
- Revenue shows an upward trend with seasonal variations.
- A small number of products generate a significant share of revenue.
- The UK is the dominant market for sales.
- High-volume products are not always the highest revenue generators.

Recommendations
- Improve inventory planning for peak months.
- Expand product offerings to reduce revenue concentration risk.
- Focus on growing non-UK markets.
- Promote high-value products to maximize revenue.
